FRONT END APPLICITY

You're probably thinking "This guy doesn't know how to spell!" or "Is 'Applicity' really a word?" No need to reach for your dictionary, it isn't a real word. It's simply my approach to front-end design. I believe that applicable simplicity should drive design. End-users don't want to spend time trying to figure out how to use an application when they need to be putting that app to use!
